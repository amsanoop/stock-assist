Security Policy

Our commitment to security and responsible disclosure.

Reporting Security Issues

We take security seriously. If you find a vulnerability, please report it responsibly.

Email: security@yourdomain.com

What to Include
  • Description of the vulnerability
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • Potential impact
  • Suggestions for mitigation
Our Commitment
  • Acknowledge receipt within 24 hours
  • Provide regular updates
  • Notify you when the issue is fixed
  • Acknowledge your responsible disclosure (if desired)
Scope

This policy applies to all StockAssist services at yourdomain.com.

Out of scope:

  • Denial of Service attacks
  • Spam or social engineering attacks
  • Third-party services/websites
Safe Harbor

We consider security research under this policy to be authorized and compliant.

  • Authorized per the Computer Fraud and Abuse Act (CFAA)
  • Exempt from DMCA restrictions
  • Compliant with data protection regulations

We will not pursue legal action against researchers who:

  • Make good faith efforts to avoid privacy violations
  • Avoid disruption to our services
  • Do not exploit vulnerabilities beyond necessary proof of concept
Contact

Security concerns: security@yourdomain.com

General inquiries: support@yourdomain.com
